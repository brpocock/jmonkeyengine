To install the Blender jME XML Exporter, just extract the zip distribution
file into your Blender scripts directory.

Depending on whether you did a shared or personal installation of Blender,
this directory may be at C:\Program Files\Blender Foundation\Blender\.blender,
or
C:\Documents and Settings\YOURNAME\Application Data\Blender Foundation\Blender\.blender
or %HOME%\.blender.
