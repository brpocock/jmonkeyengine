To install the Blender jME XML Exporter, just extract the zip distribution
file into your Blender scripts directory.

Depending on whether you did a shared or personal installation of Blender and
your Windows variant, this directory may be at
 C:\Program Files\Blender Foundation\Blender\.blender,
 C:\Documents and Settings\YOURNAME\Application Data\Blender Foundation\Blender\.blender,
 C:\Users\YOURNAME\AppData\Roaming\Blender Foundation\Blender
 %HOME%\.blender.
